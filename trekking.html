Cappadocia Trekking Tours

Lycian Way most known trekking trail of Turkey and in voted 10 best trekking routes of the worl. This trekking tour which follows east part of Lycian peninsula offer much more than a trekking tour with it is ancient cities which lies by beatiful coast of Mediterianen, charming villages on the skirts of Taurus mountains.

Alexander the Great passed from this area when he made the big campaign to the east and he fell in love with the area and spend a winter here.You will enjoy scenery of the Olympos national park, which, ancient remains of Olympos city, a river and and a beatiful beach nest together with hormony.

There are 2 best season of this tour.Spring and Autumn. Spring may more spectacular with green grasses, flowers and blue sky. April to June best spring season. September&October more quite and sea is warmer. Possible walk any time of the year on this trail but we recommend spring and autumn.

Program details;

1.Day:Meeting in Kayseri airport. Drive to Goreme.Halfday walking at Pasabağları-Vaftizci Yahya Church-Cavusini.3 hrs relaxed walking best view of Cappadocia.Pension.
2.Day: Akvadi-Uchisar-Guvercinlik.Today we walk 6 hours between spectacular landscape formation.Lunch at Uchisar. Back to the pension.
3.Day: Ayvali-Gomeda-Pancarlik.
4.Day: Underground city.
5.Day: Meskendir-Kizilcukur
We start walking in pine forrest and mostly ascending. Reaching the shepperd cottages with beautiful view of the valley and having lunch packs here.Visiting Old Olympos City on top of Musa Mountain. After 6 hours walking, 17 km, we arrive Adrasan Bay. Max altitude 800 meter. Pension Adrasan.
6.Day: Relaxing day
You can lie at the beach of Adrasan and relax. Or taking a daily boat trip an optional around Adrasan Bay. Pension Adrasan.
7.Day: Adrasan - Gelidonia Lighthouse
Todays walk is one of the most beatiful walks of all trail. It start inside the pineforest fallow blue mediteranean coast.Gelidonia Lighthouse is enchanting and the view from the lighthouse at the break time is great. This cape was very important and the most feared cape of time in ancient times. Today many ancient times wreck lies under the sea and found oldes wreck in the world at this nose. 6 hrs walkig, 17 km. We 45 min. drive back to Adrasan. Pension Adrasan.
8. Day: End of the tour.
Transfer to the Antalya Airport and fly home.

Iran Demavand Tours

Program details: 1.Day: Arrival to Tehran airport. Transfer to our hotel and sightseeing after some rest. Covered Bazaar, National Treasure & Jewelry Museum. Hotel-Tehran
2.Day: 2.5 hrs driving to Reineh village -Damavand and start trek to the first camp Goosfand Sara at 3100 meters altitude. Possible to take a vehicle until here too. Camp 1
3.Day: We trek up to Bargah Sevom camp2 at 4150 m. 4 hrs walking. Camp 2
4.Day: Summit Day! Wake up early in the morning, and possibly in the night, to start to summit attemp.After 6-8 hrs walking on easy trail we may reach to summit 5671 meter. Descent to our Camp2

5.Day: After breakfast, we descend from camp2 and transfer to the Tehran hotel. In case of bad weather we may try to summit 2nd time and descend down all the way down to Tehran.
6.Day: Full day sigtseeing. We will also have time for shopping .Hotel-Tehran
7.Day: Transfer to airport end of the tour.

Kazbeg-Georgia Trekking Tours

Mt Kazbegi,5047m,is one of the highest,most beautiful and popular peak of Caucasus Range.
Reaching to Kazbegi Village after 3 hrs spectacular view driving. Dare to see old church symbol of Kazbegi.

Interesting part of this trip walking on Gergeti Glacier close to cravasses and crossing them. Nice to staying old meteo station-changed as a mountain hut at 3700 meter.You can watch amazing view of Caucasus mountains from here. Route it not really technical but need to use crampons and tying ropes for hidded cravasses.
Different culture, good food and traditional drinks.Enjoy it! Tour start-finish in Tbilisi.

Program details;

1.Day : Tbilisi
We meet in Tbilisi Airport capital of Georgia. Transfer to the hotel in Tbilisi.Sightseeing.Hotel Tbilisi.

2.Day: Kazbegi Village.
3 hrs mininbus driving to the Kazbegi Village-1750 m. Half day walking around village and visiting to old church symbol of Kazbegi.Pension Kazbegi.

3.Day : Meteo Station-Mountain Hut 3700m
5-6 hrs walking to the old meteo station from the village. Old meteo station changed as a mountain hut many years ago. Our luggages will be carried by the horses until glacier 3500m. After this point we carry our backpacks to the meteo station 3700m.

4.Day: Aclimatization day.
We can climb to 4260 m high peak. It is not a technical but still need to use crampons. 4-5 hrs return trip.Back to meteo station.
5.Day : Summit 5047m &Back to the Meteo Staion
Early in the morning we leave from mountain hut.5-7 hrs walking on glacier.May need rope tying while walking on the glacier cause of hidded cravasses.Last part of the summit route 40 degrees need to use crampons.We back to mountain hut 3800m.

6.Day : Back to Tbilisi
We leave from meteo station and arrive to Kazbegi Village after 4-5 hrs walking.Having a beer brand Kazbegi in the village and drive back to Tbilisi.Hotel Tbilisi.

7.Day : Full day sightseeing in Tbilisi.

8.Day : Transfer to the airport.End of the tour.
