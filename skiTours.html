Artos-Suphan-Ararat Ski Tours

3 spectacular eastern summits with skies! Mt.Artos 3550m, Mt Suphan 4058, Mt.Ararat 5137m. Ski season start in middle of March until end of May. Usually snow start from 2100 m around Van Lake area. Suphan is an old volcano. Snow condition is excellent quality, fresh powder sometimes hard. Weather condition can be cold and windy sometimes. Possible to reach until summit with skies and downhill from those summits.

This program created for who want to meet in Van and like to see some sightseeing in Van and Dogubeyazit . Includes 4 night hotel and 3 night camping on Mt.Ararat. Tour start-finish in Van.

Program details;

1.Day: Meeting with our guide in Van airport and transfer to our hotel. Half day sightseeing in Van. Akdamar Island and Van Castle. Briefing about program in the hotel.Hotel-Van 2.Day: We drive to Gevas 45 km for Mt Artos. Vehicle reach 1900 m. We start to walking from here .After 3-4 hrs ski walking we reach to the summit 3550m. 1hr downhill. After meeting with our vehicle we drive 140 km to Adilcevaz the town close to Suphan. Hotel-Adilcevaz. 3.Day: Leaving early in the morning from hotel 30 min drive to Kiskili Village-2400m. 5-7 hrs ski walking to the summit 4058m. 2 hrs downhill from perfect conditioned snow. 2.5 hrs driving to the Dogubeyazit -180 km. Hotel-Dogubeyazit 4.Day: We load our luggages to our vehicle and than 50 minute driving to Eli Village 2200m. We start walking from here without skies. Our luggages and skies carried by horses. After 2 hrs walking we arrive 2900m.Camp1
5.Day: We pack our luggages giving them to porters and than start to ski walking for camping at high Camp2. High camp is based 3750m. Camp2
6.Day: We start early in the morning for summit attempt. Daylight comes at 4-4.30 about. After 5-7 hrs ski walking we reach to the summit 5137m. Downhill to Camp2. We pack our stuff again after lunch and than descend to Camp1. (If the group in a good shape we can go direct to the Dogubeyazit)
7.Day: 2 hrs descent to Eli Village meeting with our vehicle . Driving to Van and visiting Muradiye Waterfall on the way back to Van. We come our hotel in Van. Hotel-Van
8.Day:Transfer to Van airport. End of the tour.

Eastern Anatolia - Erzincan Ski Tours

Day touring from a hotel base

Erzincan is a city, located directly on the Silk Road. The city lies at 1.200 m height and rugged and magnificent mountains surrounded the city like a wall. Munzur mountain range, steep and around a dozen summit over 3000 meters. Erzincan has warmer climate compare to other eastern mountains. Weather that does not changes frequently. In Spring it is usually mild with day time temperatures of 8-15°C. Snow level mostly start from 1800 meter in spring time. Best skiing period start in mid March until end of April.
Program includes 7 nights hotel. Tour start-finish in Istanbul.

Program details;

1.Day: Meeting at Istanbul Airport. Transfer to the hotel close to sigtseeing area. Half day sigtseeing if you have early flight. Visiting Sultanahmet Mosque&Ayasofia.Overnight in the hotel. Istanbul Hotel.
2.Day: We transfer to the airport and flight to Erzincan. Transfer to the hotel and half day sigtseeing around the city. Erzincan hotel

3.Day: Mt. Ergan (3287 m) Mt Ergan is located in the south of Erzincan. 40 minutes driving from our hotel to the village where we start to walking. We may walk a little bit until snow level and put the skies on around 1800 meters. Fallowing north face of the peak for ascending and downhill from one of the longest and superb face.5 hrs ascent-2 hrs downhill. Drive back to the hotel. Erzincan hotel.
4.Day: Mt. Akbaba (3445 m)Mt Akbaba is in the same range with Ergan mountain and second highest point of the range. It is going to be longest skiing day. Mostly snow level starts from 1800 meter. 25-30 degrees steep. 5 hrs ascent-2.5 hrs downhill. Drive back to the hotel. Erzincan hotel.

5.Day: Mt.Munzur Summit (3300m) Today we drive bit longer-1.5 hrs- to our starting point at the skirts of Mt Munzur. 4 hrs ascent-2 hrs downhill. Munzur mountain gives you the one of the best skiing day with wide slops and snow quality. Drive back to the hotel.Erzincan hotel. 6.Day: Mt. Hel (3.320 m)
45 min.driving to the south of Erzincan and than start to ski walking from 1800 meter. 4 hrs ascend-2 hrs downhill. 25-30 degree steep. Drive back to the hotel. Erzincan hotel.

7. Day: Skitour Mt. Esence (3.550 m)
It is going to be last and highest peak of the area. We drive 1 hr to place where we start to ski walking .We may choose the south or north side of the mountain depends on snow and weather condition. 5 hrs ascent-2.5 hrs downhill. Drive back to the hotel. Erzincan hotel

8.Day: We drive early in the morning to the Erzincan and than fligth to Istanbul. End of the tour.

Taurus Volcanos Ski Tours

Day touring from a mountain hut&hotel base
The Aladaglar is part of the Taurus range have over 3700 meter high. There is beautiful landscape to in the region,including the pine forest, karst rock formation, pretty waterfalls and underground streams. Rocky limestone range have perfect snow condition in February-March and gives you ideal ski touring. We choose most spectacular skiing routes of Taurus Valleys.
While climbing from a valley not uncommon to observe wild life (wolf, lynx, ibex).
Mt.Hasan 3265 m&Mt.Erciyes 3916 m both old volcanos of Middle Anatolia and have spectacular views.
Program includes 4 nights mountain hut and 3 nights hotel. Tour start-finish in Kayseri.

Program details;

Day 1: We meet at the airport in Kayseri and than drive to Nigde-Cukurbag Village.(2.5 hrs-175 km). Briefing about tour program before dinner.Cukurbag-Hut

Day 2: Pancarli 2900m. It will be warm up day with 2900 meter summit Pancarli.20 min.transfer to Buldurus pass.1720m. Skinning to Pancarli summit .4 hrs ascent-1 hr descent. Back to the Cukurbag-Hut.

Day 3: Emler 3725m.20 min transfer to Sokullupinar Valley. We start walking with skies from 1800 m fallowing Karayalak Valley up to 3723 m summit Emler. Emler is second highest peak of Taurus range.Possible to see all Taurus range and Mt.Erciyes.7-9 hrs return trip. Cukurbag-Hut

Day 4: Little Alaca 3265m.Summit Little Alaca(Kortekli) day. Reaching to Sarimemetler 1775 m with vehicle and fallowing Mangirci Valley . We gain 1500m to the summit 3265 m. 6-7 hrs return trip. Cukurbag-Hut

Day 5: Before the Mt.Hasan summit day we drive 2 hrs to Ihlara Valley and half day sigtseeing in fabolous landscape. Ihlara-Pension

Day 6: Hasan 3268m. 40 min drive to Helvadere Village from our pension. We start skinning from 1800 meter. After 5 hrs skinning reaching to the summit Hasan 3265m. After descending we drive 2 hrs to Kayseri for Mt.Erciyes.Kayseri-Hotel.

Day 7: Erciyes 3916m.After a 35 min drive we arrive to Tekir . We start to walking with skies from 2200 m fallowing North-East route 6-8 hrs return trip. Summit is 3916m. Kayseri-Hotel

Day 8: Transfer to Kayseri airport. End of the tour.
