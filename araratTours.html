Mt.Ararat 7 Days Tours

Ararat is Turkey's highest mountain with 5137m and is located at the border with Iran and Armenia. Ararat is an impressive snowy volcano. For several years, Ararat closed for trekking and ascents, but at 1998 opened the mountain again.

We climb to summit from Dogubeyazit classic south route. From here, we will attempt the climb after placing two high camps. Crampon, self arrest and basic snow walking techniques are essential. Ararat is not a technical peak but cold winds and high altitude make this an excellent choice for climbers interested in making an ascent of this historical peak.

Mt Ararat 7 day program created for the ones, who want to meet in Van and like to see some sightseeing in Van and Dogubeyazit. Includes 2 night hotel and 4 night camping on Mt.Ararat. Tour start-finish in Van.

Program details;

1.Day: Meeting with our guide in Van airport and than we drive to Dogubeyazit by private vehicle. 180 km 2,5 hrs. Visiting Ishakpasa Palace after lunch in Dogubeyazit. Briefing about climbing program to Ararat in the hotel. Hotel-Dogubeyazit
2.Day: We load our luggages to our vehicle and than 50 minute driving to Eli Village 2200m. We start walking from here with our daypacks. Our luggages carried by horses until end of the tour. After 3-4 hrs walking we arrive 3300m.Camp1
3.Day: Acclimatization day. We start to walking after relaxed breakfast to Camp2-4170m. 3-4 hrs ascent -2 hrs descent. We will have packed lunch and having them at Camp2. We descend back to Camp1.
4.Day: We load all our camping stuff on horses and than start to walking for camping at high camp. High camp is based 4170m. Camp2
5.Day: We start very early in the morning for summit attempt. Daylight comes 4-4.30 about. After 5-6 hrs walking we reach to the summit 5137m. Descend to Camp2. We pack our stuff again after lunch and than descend to Camp1.
6.Day: 3 hrs descent to Eli Village meeting with our vehicle . Driving to Van and visiting Muradiye Waterfall on the way back to Van. We come our hotel in Van and half day sightseeing. Akdamar Island, Van Castle. Hotel-Van
7.Day:Transfer to Van airport. End of the tour.

Climbing permit
Ararat is in a second degree military zone. Because of Ararat's location all foreign climbers who want to climb need to get a climbing permission by the agencies before they come.
