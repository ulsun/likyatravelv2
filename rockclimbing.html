Rock Climbing in Olympos&Geyikbayiri

Antalya region getting more famous climbing destination in climbers society. Geyikbayiri and Olympos are most attractive climbing locations in Turkey and the world. There are more than 1000 bolted sport routes on limestone.
Geyikbayiri have more than 750,Olympos have 250 routes.

We offer private guiding especially in Olympos&Geyikbayiri and also in the surrounding area. There are more fun in Olympos.You can combine trip as climbing and some relaxing at the beach.We also offer deep water solo and hierage of climbing equipment.
Our tours ranging from half day to multiple day trips and can tailor these depending your climbing experience.
All our guides have Sport Climbing Trainer&Instructor Certificate from TMF and they are local climbers speaking foreign languages.

Deep Water Solo - DWS

Deep Water Soloing mostly known as water bouldering or psicoblock. No any protection gear using in this style. Just a climbing shoes need it. Climbers reaching the DWS routes with sea kayak or with a boat. Water must be deep enoug for safe falling. Also you have to know your limit. It meant you should decide how high you climb and can jump or fall from the routes.
It is really fun climbing and challenging.
