Private Guiding

We can organize any of the treks, ski tours or courses on this website on a private basis for groups of friends, families, clubs, charities or any other group. You decide when you want to go, then we make the arrangements for you. This gives you the advantage of having complete control over your trip, without having the administrative headache of the organization.

The cost of going private can be surprisingly reasonable, particularly if you take the self-led option. The larger the group, the less the cost per person, and considerable savings can be made over the price of comparable brochure trips.

You can be sure that you will be with very experienced, skillfull and speaks fluently English and some other foreign languages tour guides. All our guides have min. 15 years experiences at outdoor tours and member of Turkish Federations. Guides are skillfull about using necessary techniques, GPS&Maps, First Aid Trained and Search&Rescue Team members of associations.
